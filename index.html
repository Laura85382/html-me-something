Dinosaur Road Trip

Spring Break 2014

Inspired by a recommended road trip in "Sunset" magazine, this is the story of our funfilled dinosaur road trip - The wacky adventures of a couple of dinosaur crazy families over Spring Break!! Keep reading to learn all about our adventures over four states in seven days. Plenty of Dino tales (& footprints), along with a big Dam and a real life ghost town!

Fun Facts along the Way

Just a few goofy notes about our trip.

  • The highlight for the kids of the Hoover Dam trip was feeding the fish at Lake Mead.
  • The stop in St. George at the Dino Track museum was a complete fluke. Saw a sign and stopped. Awesome little museum.
  • The Natural History Museum in Salt Lake City is the best one I have ever visited (and nerd alert - I have been to quite a few.)
  • The kids were thrilled to play in a little patch of snow at Bryce Canyon National Park.
  • My daughter puked at the Nationl Dinosaur Monument (bad hot dog).
  • There was a McDonalds right next to the Dino museum in Fruita, CO. Not sure which one the kids were more excited about.
  • One of the best pictures of the trip, was all seven kids sitting in rocking chairs in front of a shop in the Ghost town.
  • There were no crowds on our trip, as our school district had different Spring Break week then the Utah schools.
  • My daughter, in her "I set my own style" manner, wore the same crazy hat everyday.
  • The kids qualified as "Junior Rangers" at all three National Parks!
  • In the little downtime we had, played board games, swam at the hotel pool and even saw a movie.
